Pizza Recipe

This is a picture of the final product! The World's best pizza.

Did you know that 5 billion pizzas are sold worldwide each year? 36% of all pizzas will be topped wit pepperoni.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh pizza dough
  • Italian tomatoe sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Spread flour onto the pizza stone
  2. Stretch the pizza dough and roll it out into a circle atop the pizza stone
  3. Add your desired amount of Italian tomatoe sauce. Remember: Less is more
  4. Cover the pizza with a heavy layer of fresh Mozzarella cheese. Ensure that the sauce is covered
  5. Add 10-12 pepperoni slices to the top of the pizza
  6. Bake in a preheated oven for 12-14 minutes at 400 degrees fahrenheit
