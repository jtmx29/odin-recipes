Cookie Recipe

This is a picture of the final product! The World's best cookie.

Did you know that 5 billion cookies are sold worldwide each year? 36% of all cookies will include chocolate chips.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Add the butter and sugar together in the same bowl. Mix the two ingredients until fully combined
  2. Add in two eggs and slowly beat mixture together on the low setting while incorporating the flour slowly
  3. Add in chocolate chips and handmix
  4. Line a sheet pan with parchment paper
  5. Scoop 1Tbsp sized balls of cookie dough onto the parchment paper. Be sure to separate each cookie dough ball by at least 2 inches
  6. Bake in a preheated oven for 8 minutes at 350 degrees fahrenheit
