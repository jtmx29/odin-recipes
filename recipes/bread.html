Bread Recipe

This is a picture of the final product! The World's best bread.

Did you know that 5 billion loafs of bread are sold worldwide each year? 36% of all bread will include wheat bran.

Ingredients:

  • Wheat flour
  • Bran
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Add the butter and bran together in the same bowl
  2. Add in two eggs and slowly beat mixture together on the low setting while incorporating the flour slowly
  3. Add in one quarter cup of warm water
  4. Let the bread dough rest for 1 hour in the bowl
  5. Add the bread dough mixture to a bread pan that has been greased with butter
  6. Bake in a preheated oven for 50 minutes at 300 degrees fahrenheit
